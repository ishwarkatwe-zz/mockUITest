Why use Inspec Plus?


Platform of subject matter experts

The Inspec Plus is the authoritative source of information about advances in the causes, diagnosis, treatment and prevention of cancer.

Advanced Searches

The programs and services of the Inspec Plus foster the exchange of knowledge and new ideas among scientists dedicated to cancer research, provide training opportunities for the next generation of cancer researchers, and increase public understanding of cancer.

Trend Analysis

In conjunction with the publication of its fifth annual Cancer Progress Report, Inspec Plus commissioned a national survey on cancer and cancer research funding.
